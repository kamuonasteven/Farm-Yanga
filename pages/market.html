💡 Selling Tips for Malawian Farmers
📅 Timing matters — Prices are lowest at harvest (March–May). Store your grain and sell in August–October when prices rise by 30–50%.
🤝 Form groups — Farmer cooperatives negotiate better prices and reduce transport costs. Selling together gives you bargaining power.
📊 Compare markets — ADMARC sets a floor price. Open markets usually pay more, especially for high-quality produce. Check both.
🌱 Grade your produce — Clean, dry grains at the right moisture (13%) command premium prices. Invest in drying and sorting.
